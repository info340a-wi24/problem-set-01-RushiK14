Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a popular superhero from the Marvel comic book series "Spider-Man". His origin story involves getting bit by a radioactive spider and gaining superhuman powers. He uses his powers to swing around New York City, fighting criminals and saving civilians from dangerous situations.

Why I Admire This Character:

  • He's super intelligent, having created his own web shooters, other gadgets and also at times cures for villains like Lizard.
  • He's very kind and compassionate. He's always trying to help others, both as Spider-Man and as Peter Parker.
  • He's incredibly resilient. Spider-Man experiences many losses in his life, but perseveres and always has a positive mindset.
